Introductory Question

Learning Objectives

In this course, you'll discover four key principles that can change your life. Think of them as arrows to hit the target of a meaningful relationship with God.

Law 1: God's Love

Understand God's unconditional love and His wonderful plan for your life.

Law 2: Man's Sin

Recognize that sin separates us from God, preventing us from experiencing His love.

Law 3: God's Provision

Discover that Jesus Christ is God's only solution for our sin.

Law 4: Our Response

Learn how to personally receive Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord.

First Law: God Loves You and Offers a Wonderful Plan for Your Life

Key Biblical Truth

God's love for you is not a vague concept; it's an active, personal, and unconditional love.

Bible Verse

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." - John 3:16 (NIV)

Illustration

Imagine a master artist who creates a beautiful painting. The artist doesn't just create it and walk away; they cherish it, protect it, and desire for it to be seen and appreciated. In the same way, God, the master creator, has a deep and abiding love for you, His creation. He has a plan for your life that is more beautiful and fulfilling than you can imagine.
