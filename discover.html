Discover Jesus: A Personal and Interactive Story

The Grand Narrative: From Creation to Redemption

From the beginning of time, God created a perfect world, filled with beauty and harmony. But humanity chose to turn away from Him, breaking that relationship and introducing pain and separation into the world.

Yet, God's love is so great that He always had a plan to restore this broken relationship. This plan is centered on one person: Jesus.

Your Questions, His Answers

Jesus died on the cross to pay the price for our sins. His death is an act of infinite love, allowing anyone who believes in Him to have eternal life and a restored relationship with God.

See: John 3:16, Romans 5:8

Knowing God personally begins with faith in Jesus Christ. You can speak to Him through prayer, read the Bible for Him to know more, and seek to live according to His teachings. The Holy Spirit will guide you.

See: John 14:6, James 4:8
